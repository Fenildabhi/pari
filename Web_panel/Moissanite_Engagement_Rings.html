If you are curious if diamonds are the only worthy jewel for your marriage proposal, the answer is “no.” In fact, many other stones will look as beautiful and worthy enough to give to the special lady in your life. One of those stones is moissanite, which looks like a diamond, but is not as hard. There is currently a growing trend where men give precious stones other than diamonds for practical and aesthetic reasons. Many couples are now choosing unique moissanite engagement rings rather than diamonds, not just because they’re less expensive, but also because of their amazing properties. The stones in this jewelry are almost as hard as diamond, earning a 9.25 on Mohs Scale of Hardness. It almost approaches the hardness of diamonds, which register a 10. It is definitely one of the hardest natural materials on the planet and it will surely last a long time. If you want a diamond ring, but don’t have the budget for it, you can choose moissanite engagement rings instead. These gems have other amazing properties, which can compete even with diamonds. These gems give you rainbow sparkles and the bigger the stone, the more intense the colors. Another interesting fact about moissanite engagement rings is that the stone used here can be very hard to find in nature. These are often created in a laboratory, which means that you are not destroying the environment or supporting illegal activities in any way. Moissanite engagement rings are definitely not a second choice for couples. Many people simply prefer the fiery rainbow flashes these gems produce when hit by light. Being more affordable than a diamond engagement ring is certainly an additional advantage. These gems look brilliant and beautiful no matter what metal the band it is placed. We have them in yellow gold, white gold, rose gold, or palladium. You and your partner will surely fall in love with them too if you see our collection. They are made with the finest craftsmanship.
