Home Price Match Guarantee

At Allurez we want to make sure you are getting the very best price on your diamond purchase. Match Guarantee program makes it easy for you to compare the price of a competitors diamond with one of our diamonds. The diamonds must be of the same cut, clarity, color, and carat weight. All you need to get started is a link to our competitors website offer or published price offer. The following are the guidelines of the Allurez Price Match Guarantee:

  • The competitors diamond may be graded at any major gem lab. Only reports from the same gem lab can be compared. For example, if the competitor used GIA to grade the diamond, than we would have to use GIA as well. This would be a fair comparison.
  • This offer does not apply to promotional pricing or privately discounted items. We will match our price with the competitors publicly listed price.
  • You can match an unlimited amount of stones. We want to make sure that you are getting the best price for your jewelry.
  • This price match does not apply to customized products.
