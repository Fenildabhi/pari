Gemstone Wedding Rings Gemstone wedding bands are a bright alternative to diamond settings. At Allurez, we carry all the gemstones you desire in a variety of metals and settings. These rings include eternity, half-eternity, and many other settings. With more than a thousand gemstone wedding bands available, you are sure to find a ring that suits your style. Gemstone wedding bands are available in many different categories, some of which overlap. For example, many eternity styles are also stackable, and many antique styles also include pave settings. Other styles are completely unique, such as our contoured halo rings, and every category includes many great options. In addition to wedding bands, we also offer gemstone anniversary rings, which are a perfect way to express your love. They are ideal for any special occasion in addition to anniversaries, as well, and they can be worn with other rings or separately. Anniversary styles are available with many different stones, settings, and metals. They often include traditional white diamonds in addition to gemstones for a luxurious touch. Antique styles are perfect for traditionalists who prefer a new ring with an old-timey charm. Antique-style rings are often more ornate than contemporary styles, making them an ideal setting for colorful jewels. Intricate engravings are another feature of these antique styles, and they look great with any color metal. Eternity rings allow you to express your never-ending love, and they look great with colorful gems. Both prong and channel settings are available in eternity styles. Prong settings offer a more traditional look, while channel settings feature stones set in a channel of metal that runs along the top and bottom of the ring. In addition to full eternity styles, many of our fashion rings are half-eternity styles. Five-stone and halo settings are a unique complement to your engagement ring. Halo rings in particular are contoured in such a way that they create a halo around your engagement solitaire. Therefore, these rings are ideal for stacking. Five stone rings, on the other hand, feature five prominent stones, which you can match to your engagement ring. These are only a few of the styles available at Allurez. Others include pave, side stone accented, and stackable rings; all of which are beautiful.
